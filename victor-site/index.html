work

the kinds of problems i like working on.

not portfolio case studies in the usual sense. more like a map of the domains and system questions i keep returning to.

01 / AI systems

applying AI where trust, risk, and operational accuracy matter.

i’m interested in AI systems that support real decisions: fraud detection, intelligent analysis, workflow automation, and systems that improve outcomes without adding noise.

02 / cybersecurity

understanding how security shapes confidence, behavior, and adoption.

cybersecurity is not only technical. it changes how people trust systems, how organizations respond to risk, and how digital products succeed or fail.

03 / information systems

studying the systems behind digital transformation.

i’m drawn to adoption, usability, governance, and the question of how technology creates measurable outcomes inside institutions and markets.

04 / cloud architecture

building infrastructure that scales quietly and holds up under pressure.

i care about resilient systems, sensible architecture choices, and cloud foundations that make products easier to secure, maintain, and extend.
