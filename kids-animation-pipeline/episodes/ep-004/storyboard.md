# Episode 004 Storyboard — Mimi and the Missing Blue Kite

## Scene 1
- Duration: 40 seconds
- Shot type: Wide park shot
- Camera movement: Follow the kite as it rises
- Character action: Friends run happily with kite
- Emotion: Excitement, anticipation
- Environment/props: Park, grass, tree, kite string

## Scene 2
- Duration: 35 seconds
- Shot type: Tracking and reaction close-ups
- Camera movement: Quick follow on the kite, then settle
- Character action: Wind pulls kite away, friends react
- Emotion: Surprise, concern without panic
- Environment/props: Flying kite, wind leaves

## Scene 3
- Duration: 55 seconds
- Shot type: Search montage and clue close-ups
- Camera movement: Pan from ground clues to upward glance
- Character action: Friends inspect the park and talk through clues
- Emotion: Focus, teamwork
- Environment/props: Ribbon bits, tree branches, bench

## Scene 4
- Duration: 50 seconds
- Shot type: Reveal shot of kite in branch, then wide happy ending
- Camera movement: Tilt up to reveal kite, then pull back to group
- Character action: Friends safely retrieve kite and cheer
- Emotion: Relief, joy
- Environment/props: Tree branch, kite
