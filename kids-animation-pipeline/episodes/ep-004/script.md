# Episode 004 Script — Mimi and the Missing Blue Kite

## Lesson
Looking carefully and asking friends for help can solve problems.

## Runtime Target
3.5 minutes

## Scene 1
- Setting: Windy park
- Visual action: Mimi holds a bright blue kite and smiles.
- Dialogue:
  - Mimi: "It's a perfect day to fly my kite!"
  - Bobo: "Let's run!"
  - Lulu: "Up, up, up!"
- Narration: Mimi was ready for a fun kite day with her friends.
- Music/SFX: Breezy cheerful music

## Scene 2
- Setting: Same park
- Visual action: A gust of wind pulls the kite away.
- Dialogue:
  - Mimi: "Oh! My kite!"
  - Bobo: "I'll look everywhere!"
  - Mimi: "Let's slow down and look for clues."
- Narration: The blue kite was gone, but Mimi did not panic.
- Music/SFX: Wind gust, surprise sound

## Scene 3
- Setting: Around the park
- Visual action: Friends find blue ribbon bits and look upward.
- Dialogue:
  - Lulu: "I see something blue!"
  - Bobo: "Is it over there?"
  - Mimi: "Let's look closely."
- Narration: The friends searched carefully, noticing color, shape, and tiny hints.
- Music/SFX: Soft searching music

## Scene 4
- Setting: Tree branch in the park
- Visual action: The kite is found gently caught in a low branch. Friends get it down safely.
- Dialogue:
  - Bobo: "There it is!"
  - Mimi: "We found it together."
  - Lulu: "Hooray for careful looking!"
- Narration: Calm thinking and teamwork helped the friends solve the problem.
- Music/SFX: Happy resolution music

## Ending Recap
- Lesson summary: Looking carefully and asking for help can make problems feel smaller.
- Final upbeat line: "Let's look closely!"
