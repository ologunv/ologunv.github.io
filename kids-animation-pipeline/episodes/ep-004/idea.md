# Episode 004 — Mimi and the Missing Blue Kite

## Title
Mimi and the Missing Blue Kite

## Lesson
Looking carefully and asking friends for help can solve problems.

## Runtime
3.5 minutes

## Storyline
On a windy day, Mimi’s favorite blue kite disappears just before flying time. Bobo wants to run everywhere at once, but Mimi suggests they look carefully for clues. The friends search the park, noticing color, shape, and little signs along the way. They finally find the kite gently caught in a tree branch, and Mimi teaches everyone that calm thinking can help when something feels lost.
