# Episode 003 — Tiko Counts the Farm Friends

## Title
Tiko Counts the Farm Friends

## Lesson
Counting can help us stay calm and solve small problems.

## Runtime
3 minutes

## Storyline
Tiko visits the farm and is asked to count the little ducklings before snack time. But the ducklings keep waddling around, and Tiko loses track. Instead of panicking, Tiko counts slowly and carefully with help from Bobo and Lulu. The friends learn numbers together from one to ten, and Tiko shows that going slowly can be a smart way to get things right.
