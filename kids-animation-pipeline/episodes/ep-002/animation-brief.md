# Episode 002 Animation Brief — Lulu and the Bedtime Stars

## Style Notes
- Warm evening palette
- Slower pacing than daytime episodes
- Cozy bedroom and bedtime atmosphere

## Scene Notes
### Scene 1
- Lulu's movement should feel playful, not wild
- Set contrast between excitement and calm bedtime energy

### Scene 2
- Mimi should feel grounding and reassuring
- Keep step-by-step gestures very clear for young viewers

### Scene 3
- Make routine visually satisfying
- Consider rhythmic edit pattern for tidy, brush, pajamas

### Scene 4
- Stars should feel soothing and magical
- Final bed settle should be gentle and quiet

## Reused Assets
- Lulu rig
- Mimi rig
- Bedroom environment

## New Assets Needed
- Bedtime chart
- Star glow window effect
