# Episode 002 — Lulu and the Bedtime Stars

## Title
Lulu and the Bedtime Stars

## Lesson
Bedtime routines help us feel calm and ready for sleep.

## Runtime
3.5 minutes

## Storyline
Lulu is having so much fun that she does not want to get ready for bed. She flutters from toy to toy and keeps saying, "One more game!" Mimi helps turn the bedtime routine into a gentle game: tidy toys, brush teeth, put on pajamas, and count glowing stars. By the end, Lulu feels cozy and sleepy, and bedtime becomes something soft and comforting instead of something to avoid.
