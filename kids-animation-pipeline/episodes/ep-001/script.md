# Episode 001 Script — Bobo Learns to Share

## Lesson
Sharing can make playtime more fun for everyone.

## Runtime Target
3 minutes

## Scene 1
- Setting: Sunny playground
- Visual action: Bobo bounces a bright red ball with a big smile.
- Dialogue:
  - Bobo: "Wow! A shiny red ball!"
  - Bobo: "I want to play with it all by myself!"
  - Mimi: "Can we play too, Bobo?"
  - Tiko: "We can make a fun game together."
  - Bobo: "Maybe later. Right now it's just mine."
- Narration: Bobo was very excited about the red ball. But his friends wanted to join the fun too.
- Music/SFX: Cheerful playground music, soft bounce sounds

## Scene 2
- Setting: Same playground, near a little hill
- Visual action: Bobo tries to play alone, but the ball bounces away and rolls behind a little fence.
- Dialogue:
  - Bobo: "Boing! Boing! Wheee!"
  - Bobo: "Oh no! My ball!"
  - Bobo: "It's stuck!"
- Narration: Playing alone was not as easy as Bobo thought. Soon, the ball rolled away.
- Music/SFX: Ball bounce, rolling sound, tiny surprise sting

## Scene 3
- Setting: Fence corner at the playground
- Visual action: Mimi looks carefully while Tiko reaches slowly and safely.
- Dialogue:
  - Mimi: "Let's look closely."
  - Tiko: "Slow and smart works too."
  - Bobo: "Can you help me get it back?"
  - Mimi: "Of course, Bobo."
  - Tiko: "Friends help friends."
- Narration: Bobo asked for help, and his friends were kind and ready.
- Music/SFX: Gentle teamwork music

## Scene 4
- Setting: Open playground space
- Visual action: Tiko gets the ball back. The friends form a circle and roll the ball to each other.
- Dialogue:
  - Bobo: "Thank you, friends!"
  - Bobo: "Do you want to play together now?"
  - Mimi: "Yes!"
  - Tiko: "That sounds fun."
  - Lulu: "We can do it together!"
  - Bobo: "Sharing makes the game better!"
- Narration: Now the game was bigger, happier, and more fun for everyone.
- Music/SFX: Happy music, bouncing and giggles

## Ending Recap
- Lesson summary: Sharing helps everyone feel included and makes playtime more fun.
- Final upbeat line: "Let's try together!"
