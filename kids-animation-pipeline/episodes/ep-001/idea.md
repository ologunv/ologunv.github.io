# Episode 001 — Bobo Learns to Share

## Title
Bobo Learns to Share

## Lesson
Sharing can make playtime more fun for everyone.

## Runtime
3 minutes

## Storyline
At the playground, Bobo finds a bright red ball and wants to keep it all to himself. When Mimi and Tiko ask to play too, Bobo says no and quickly realizes that playing alone is not as fun. The ball rolls into a tricky spot, and Bobo needs help to get it back. His friends help him, and Bobo learns that sharing makes games bigger, happier, and more exciting.
