# Episode 001 Storyboard — Bobo Learns to Share

## Scene 1
- Duration: 40 seconds
- Shot type: Wide opening shot, then medium shots
- Camera movement: Gentle push-in on Bobo and ball
- Character action: Bobo bounces ball proudly, friends approach smiling
- Emotion: Excitement, curiosity
- Environment/props: Playground, red ball, sandbox, slide

## Scene 2
- Duration: 35 seconds
- Shot type: Medium shot to tracking shot
- Camera movement: Follow the ball as it rolls away
- Character action: Bobo chases the ball, stops when it gets stuck behind fence
- Emotion: Surprise, mild frustration
- Environment/props: Playground fence, grass, little hill

## Scene 3
- Duration: 45 seconds
- Shot type: Medium close-up and low angle on fence corner
- Camera movement: Slow side pan as friends work together
- Character action: Mimi points, Tiko reaches carefully, Bobo watches hopefully
- Emotion: Cooperation, focus
- Environment/props: Fence corner, ball partly visible

## Scene 4
- Duration: 60 seconds
- Shot type: Wide circle shot and close-ups
- Camera movement: Circular motion around the group while they play
- Character action: Friends roll and bounce the ball together
- Emotion: Joy, inclusion, relief
- Environment/props: Open play area, ball
