# Episode 001 Animation Brief — Bobo Learns to Share

## Style Notes
- Bright morning playground lighting
- Soft rounded motion
- Expressive but simple facial animation
- Clear readable poses for young children

## Scene Notes
### Scene 1
- Emphasize Bobo's excitement with bouncy movement
- Keep friends friendly and non-pushy
- Ball should feel visually important and attractive

### Scene 2
- Rollaway moment should feel surprising, not stressful
- Keep pacing quick but calm
- No scary stakes

### Scene 3
- Show teamwork through body language
- Mimi points, Tiko reaches slowly and safely
- Bobo should visibly soften and learn

### Scene 4
- Reward moment: group play feels visually fuller than solo play
- Use circle movement to symbolize sharing
- End with happy group pose and smile

## Reused Assets
- Playground environment
- Main cast rigs
- Basic fence prop

## New Assets Needed
- Bright red ball
