# Episode 005 — The Clean-Up Song Parade

## Title
The Clean-Up Song Parade

## Lesson
Cleaning up can be fun when everyone helps together.

## Runtime
3 minutes

## Storyline
After a big play day, toys are scattered all over the room. Nobody feels excited about cleaning up until Lulu starts a silly marching song. Soon, each friend has a job: Bobo stacks blocks, Mimi sorts colors, and Tiko puts books back neatly. The room becomes tidy little by little, and the friends celebrate with one last verse of the song.
