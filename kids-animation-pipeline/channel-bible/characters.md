# Character Guide

## Main Cast

### Bobo
- Role: curious little bear
- Personality: cheerful, eager, sometimes impatient
- Strength: always wants to help
- Weakness: rushes before thinking
- Catchphrase: "Let's try together!"

### Mimi
- Role: clever bunny friend
- Personality: calm, kind, observant
- Strength: notices details others miss
- Weakness: can be shy at first
- Catchphrase: "Let's look closely."

### Tiko
- Role: playful young turtle
- Personality: gentle, thoughtful, steady
- Strength: patient problem-solver
- Weakness: sometimes too slow for the group
- Catchphrase: "Slow and smart works too."

### Lulu
- Role: bright little bird
- Personality: energetic, musical, encouraging
- Strength: keeps everyone hopeful
- Weakness: gets distracted easily
- Catchphrase: "We can do it!"

## Character Use Rules
- keep personalities consistent
- no character should become mean just to create drama
- conflict should come from mistakes, misunderstandings, or simple obstacles
- friends solve problems together
