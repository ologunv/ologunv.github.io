# World Rules

## Setting
A soft, colorful neighborhood where the main cast can visit:
- a playground
- a little home street
- a park
- a learning garden
- a bedtime bedroom set
- a farm or animal area

## World Logic
- the world should feel safe and warm
- problems are small and understandable
- adults can exist in the background but do not need to dominate the story
- magic is okay if it helps wonder, not confusion

## Episode Pattern
1. a normal fun moment
2. a small problem appears
3. the friends try to solve it
4. they learn something
5. happy resolution and gentle recap
