# Channel Bible

## Core Direction
- Target age: 4–6
- Tone: playful, safe, gentle, bright, reassuring
- Format: short 3D animated stories with one lesson per episode
- Video length: 2.5–4 minutes
- Publishing cadence: every 3–4 days

## Content Goals
Each episode should do at least one of these:
- teach a simple life lesson
- reinforce emotional intelligence
- support early learning
- reward curiosity and imagination

## Story Rules
- one clear problem per episode
- one simple lesson per episode
- clear beginning, middle, and end
- positive ending
- repetition is good
- avoid sarcasm and complex dialogue

## Safety Rules
- no dangerous imitation behavior
- no scary villains or intense conflict
- no shaming, bullying, or humiliation as humor
- no misleading educational claims
- no chaotic, confusing, or bizarre scene changes
- no heavy branding or promotional storylines

## Visual Rules
- bright, warm color palette
- soft rounded character shapes
- clean environments
- readable expressions
- avoid overly busy backgrounds

## Character Strategy
Use a recurring cast so children become familiar with them and production becomes easier.
