# QA Checklist

## Story Quality
- Is the story easy to follow?
- Is there one main problem?
- Is the lesson clear?
- Is the ending positive?

## Age Fit
- Are words simple enough for ages 4–6?
- Are scenes calm and understandable?
- Is there repetition where helpful?

## Safety & Policy
- No unsafe behavior without correction
- No scary imagery or intense threats
- No bullying or humiliation as humor
- No deceptive educational framing
- No bizarre or confusing auto-generated feeling

## Production Readiness
- Voice lines are split clearly
- Scene transitions make sense
- Reused assets are identified
- New assets are listed
